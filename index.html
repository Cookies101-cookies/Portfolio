About Me

— bio & goals

Hey, I’m Advik Singh. I’m a sophomore at James E. Taylor High School. Ever since I was a kid, I loved tech and electrical engineering. I’ve always wanted to know how things tick: why does a circuit light up, how does a few lines of code spark something real, and what does it actually take to turn an idea into something that solves a problem? I love a good challenge that strengthens my skills. Whether I’m tinkering with electronics or diving into the latest tech trends, I’m happiest when I’m making something work. People around me say I’m enthusiastic and determined, and I think that’s what helps me turn ideas into real things. What do I want, in the big picture? I want to create technology that actually matters. Stuff that moves society forward, helps people, sparks new possibilities. So, in this portfolio, you’ll see more than just a list of projects. You’ll get a glimpse of how curious I am, how much I love learning, and how committed I am to building something better.
